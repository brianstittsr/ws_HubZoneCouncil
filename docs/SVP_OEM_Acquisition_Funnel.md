# SVP Client Acquisition Funnel
## Targeting OEM Decision Makers

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines Strategic Value Plus's client acquisition funnel specifically designed for targeting decision makers within Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs). The funnel addresses the unique challenges, pain points, and decision-making processes of key roles within OEM organizations.

---

## Target Decision Maker Personas

### Primary Decision Makers

| Role | Title Examples | Key Responsibilities | Pain Points |
|------|----------------|---------------------|-------------|
| **Supply Chain Leader** | VP Supply Chain, Director of Procurement, Chief Procurement Officer | Supplier qualification, cost management, supply continuity | Supplier quality issues, delivery delays, cost pressures |
| **Quality Executive** | VP Quality, Director of Quality Assurance, Chief Quality Officer | Quality standards, compliance, supplier audits | Non-conformances, audit failures, certification gaps |
| **Operations Leader** | VP Operations, COO, Plant Manager | Production efficiency, capacity planning, supplier performance | Production disruptions, capacity constraints, supplier reliability |
| **Engineering Leader** | VP Engineering, Director of Product Development | New product introduction, supplier technical capability | Finding capable suppliers, NPI delays, technical qualification |

### Secondary Influencers

| Role | Influence Area | Engagement Strategy |
|------|----------------|---------------------|
| **Supplier Quality Engineer** | Day-to-day supplier management | Technical content, case studies |
| **Commodity Manager** | Supplier selection, negotiations | ROI tools, competitive analysis |
| **Program Manager** | Project timelines, supplier coordination | Timeline/milestone focused content |
| **Compliance Manager** | Regulatory requirements | Certification-focused messaging |
